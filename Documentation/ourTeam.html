Logbook

Week 1: 15/02 - 21/02

Talking with the group about the project idea.

Meeting with mentor and understanding the requisites.

Organizing ideas to ask questions to the Rovisco Pais’ neuropsychologist.

Week 2: 22/02 - 28/02

Visit to the rehabilitation center Rovisco Pais and meeting with its neuropsychologist to better understand the requisites. Clear the main goal about the project and define the main templates to be used for the platform. Also understand the idea of Stroke and the physical and mental limitations of the patients.

Work on the website and documentation, organizing and preparing part of the required texts to show.

Creation of the platform.

Week 3: 01/03 - 07/03

Work on the website and documentation and correction of some parts of it.

Work on the creation of the templates and their configurations.

Creation of the platform databases and authentication.

Addition of the security entities function.

Week 4: 08/03 - 14/03

Work on the templates and focus on their configurations (possible corrections of some parts).

Integration with templates and platform

Week 5: 15/03 - 21/03

Studying voice recognition functionalities and its applications in templates.

Some changes on templates.

Addition of the prevention errors on templates.

Work on the documentation website.

Preparing the Presentation Demo.

Week 6: 22/03 - 28/03

Searching for virtual reality and Kinect functionalities and integrations of it to the theme for the platform.

Integration of voice recognition functionality on templates.

Adapting the templates to the platform (some changes on both due to of some inadaptability).

Due to of some inadaptability on templates and platform, we changed the platform by the root and started it again, but completed.

Week 7: 29/03 - 04/04

Searching for Leap functionalities, adaptations to the theme and templates.

Platform configuration on both sides, doctors and patients.

Linked databases.

Week 8: 05/04 - 11/04

Searching for new functionalities and discover how to adapt to the platform.

Design the platform and its configuration.

Platform tutorial for the Rovisco Pais’ neuropsychologists.

Week 9: 12/04 - 18/04

Leap and virtual reality functionalities: first tests.

Platform configuration.

Adaptation the statistic charts into the platform.

Week 10: 19/04 - 25/04

Creation of templates in virtual reality.

Creation of templates in leap.

Creation of new template.

Continuation of implementation of statistics.

Correction of bugs on platform.

Association of templates session to patients.

Week 11: 26/04 - 02/05

Continuation of creation of templates in virtual reality and leap.

Creation of the platform in virtual reality mode.

Correction of bugs on platform.

Week 12: 03/05 - 09/05

Configured the templates to be automatized, to be easier for the doctors to create the templates. By categorizations, they can create various templates with random images and save them. This facilitates the act of creating one by one, saving the time of its creations.

Correct some errors on the platform, discovered by users that did the usability tests and analysed with the results.

Discovered that Leap Motion is not so efficient with JavaScript. The fingers become shaken and maybe it is more difficult to the patients. We started to learn Unity and implement Leap on it.

Continue to implement VR on templates and integrate on the platform.

Continue to implement the charts and choose the best charts for the platform, by analyzing the feedback of the doctors.

Week 13: 10/05 - 17/05

The automatized templates functionality is on the platform and correct some errors to implement the best way of them.

Turning the platform easier and more user-friendly to use, visual aspect becoming better.

Develop Multiple Choice Leap Motion template.

Virtual Reality on templates and on platform better to use for patients.

Charts on the platform with the best type of charts for patients.

Week 14: 18/05 - 24/05

All configurable and automatized templates are on the platform. We also added the remove, edit and visualize functions on templates. We corrected all possible bugs and errors on the platform that we discovered and the neuropsychologist thought was better to use for them.

Added a bar on the initial login page of the patient from 0 to 10 to see how quantified is the motivation of the patient to initiate the templates. It is also very important for the patient and the doctors to see and analyze all the results after those previous sensations.

The integration of charts on platform is done and the doctors can now see the statistics for each template done by patients.
