Doctor Area

This section shows all interfaces reached by a doctor user.

Main view

Where the doctor can see his/her paitients list and take notes to each one of them. From this page, the doctor can go to Patient statistics and the Template configuration area.

Patient statistics area

This page shows all the statistics (related to a patient) to each task given.

Template configuration area

This is where the doctor can configure each task to a certain patient. The interface changes according to the task the doctor is building.

Select patient area

Templates can be used for a variety of patients. Therefore, this section allows the doctor to associate tasks to patients.

Templates and Patient Area

Bellow are the templates for each task. All the tasks are configured (images, texts, titles, etc.) by a doctor. This section also shows the patient area, since the only action they can take is to perform tasks given by the respective doctor.

Template "Categorize"

A series of images and two categories are given to the patient. All the images are related to one of the categories. The patient task is to make the Image-Category relation.

Template "Related images"

Four images and one category are given to the patient. His task is to distinguish which ones relate to the category and select them.

Template "Multiple choice"

A question and four different answers are given to the patient. One of the answers must be correct and the patients task is to select the right one.

Template "Sequence"

A shuffled sequece of images is given to the patient. His task is to correctly sort the images.

Template "Name it"

A picture is given to the patient. His task is to correctly assign a title/name to it.
